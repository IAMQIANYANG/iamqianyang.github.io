Description

This is a self-motivated group project consisting of 4 MLIS students from UBC and U of T. The purpose is to familiarize ourselves with the process of the product development.

It is a theme based city socializing APP targeted at people aged at 17-26 years old.

There are four themes: city exploration; food; pets and literature & art.We wanted to engaged this age group in meaningful social activities.
